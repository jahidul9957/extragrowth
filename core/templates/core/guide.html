Quick Guide

How to use the panel? 🧭

1

Add Funds

Go to Wallet section, scan the QR code via UPI, and submit your 12-digit UTR number.

2

Choose Service

Navigate to Services tab, select YouTube/Instagram, and check the price per 1000.

3

Place Order

Paste your public link, enter quantity, and click 'Boost Now'.

Track & Earn

Track status in 'My Orders' and invite friends to earn Free Diamonds! 💎
